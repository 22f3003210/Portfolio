Opening a new jewellery store is more than setting up interiors and inventory. It is about establishing the operational
foundation that will determine how efficiently the business functions over the coming years. My involvement begins
before operations start, ensuring every critical business function is structured from day one.
IMMEDIATE ACTION PROTOCOL
Standard Showroom SOPs & Policies: Establish
workflow standards, checklists, and daily retail
procedures.
Role Clarity & Org Structure: Define clear
departmental boundaries and daily owner check
points.
Robust Inventory Controls: Build master data,
stone/metal attributes, barcodes, and tagging
standards.
Customer CRM & Follow-up: Set up lead
management, databases, campaigns, and retention
frameworks.
HR & Payroll Governance: Implement onboarding,
role-based KPIs, attendance, and incentive models.
Governance & Approvals: Structure transaction limits,
approval hierarchies, and internal audit controls.
BI & Executive Reporting: Create daily MIS reports,
sales KPIs, and financial review systems.
Scalable Expansion Blueprint: Design the operational
foundation to support future branch expansions.
CUSTOMISED STRATEGY & ADVISORY
"Every store's system needs are unique. Based upon your spending power
(financial budget) and human capital (team capability), I will suggest the
optimal systems and tools to implement, ensuring you establish each and
everything required to run smoothly without over-engineering."
KEY STRATEGIC OBJECTIVE
"Establish role clarity, transaction boundaries, and
inventory specifications before Day 1 to ensure
scaling does not multiply operational chaos."
